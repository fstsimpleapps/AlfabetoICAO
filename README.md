# AlfabetoICAO
Privacy Policy

Alfa ICAO does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.

The app works locally on the user’s device. Text entered in the app is used only to generate the ICAO phonetic alphabet dictation and is not sent to any server.

The app does not require an account, subscription, analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.
